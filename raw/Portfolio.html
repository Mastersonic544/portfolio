№ 06 Frequent questions

The five things everyone asks.

Q.01 What's your rate? +
It depends — I scope by project, not by hour. Send me a one-paragraph brief and I'll send a number within two days. Smallest projects start around $2.4k; the average is closer to $6–10k.
Q.02 Do you work remote? +
Yes. I'm based in Sfax, GMT+1, and I overlap comfortably with EU and Maghreb hours. For US clients we'll find a window. I'll travel for kickoff weeks when it matters.
Q.03 Why "I refuse to pay for"? +
Because most SaaS is rented furniture. If a tool's logic fits in a single Postgres table and three forms, I'd rather build it once and own it than pay $29 a month forever. It's also how I learn.
Q.04 Can you teach my team? +
Probably. I've taught 100+ students full-stack and design fundamentals — both one-off workshops and month-long cohorts. Send me what you want them to walk away knowing and I'll send a syllabus.
Q.05 What's your turnaround? +
Two to four weeks for most things; longer for engineering-heavy or hardware projects. I'll tell you the honest number the day we talk, not after a deposit.
